Essential Ingredients for Authentic Sindhi Biryani

Sindhi Biryani requires a careful selection of ingredients to achieve its distinctive flavor. Here's a comprehensive list of everything you'll need to prepare this delicious dish:


Fresh ingredients for Sindhi Biryani

For the Rice:

Ingridients & Quantity

  1. Basmati Rice (long grain) 2 cups (400g).
  2. Water for boiling 8 cups.
  3. Salt 2 tablespoons.
  4. Whole Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon.
  5. Whole Cloves 4-5 pieces.
  6. Cinnamon Stick 1(2 inch pieces).
  7. Bay Leaves (2/3).

For the Meat Marinade

Ingridients & Quantity

  1. Chicken or (Beef/Muttton) 1 kg (Bone-in pieces).
  2. Yogurt 1 cup.
  3. Ginger Paste 2 tablespoons.
  4. Garlic Paste 2 tablespoons.
  5. Red Chili Powder 2 tablespoons
  6. Turmeric Powder 1 tablespoon.
  7. Garam Masala 1 tablespoon.
  8. Salt 1 tablespoon.

For the Curry Base:

Ingridients & Quantity

  1. Onions 3 large (thinly sliced).
  2. Tomatoes 3 medium (chopped).
  3. Green Chilies 6-8 (slit lengthwise).
  4. Fresh Coriander Leaves 1 bunch (chopped).
  5. Fresh Mint Leaves 1/2 bunch (chopped).
  6. Green Cardamom Pods 8-10.
  7. Black Cardamom Pods 2.
  8. Black Peppercorns 1 teaspoon.
  9. Cooking Oil 1/2 cup.

For Layering and Garnish:

Ingridients & Quantity

  1. Saffron Strands 1 pinch.
  2. Warm Milk 1/4 cup.
  3. Fried Onions 1 cup.
  4. Fresh Coriander Leaves For garnish.
  5. Fresh Mint Leaves For garnish.
  6. Lemon Wedges (2 lemons, cut into wedges).

Special Sindhi Biryani Masala (Optional):

For an authentic taste, you can prepare Sindhi Biryani Masala by dry roasting and grinding the following spices:

  • 2 tablespoons coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 5-6 cloves
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods
  • 1 black cardamom pod
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 bay leaf
